Step 4 — the key insight

XOR + AND = half adder

Two inputs, two gates in parallel, two outputs. XOR gives the Sum bit. AND gives the Carry bit.

Why two outputs? Adding 1+1 in binary gives 10 — two digits. Sum is the right digit, Carry is the left. Try it: set both A and B to 1. Sum=0, Carry=1 — that's binary 10, which equals decimal 2.
